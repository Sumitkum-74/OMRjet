FAQ

  • OMRjet is an online platform for creating, scanning, and evaluating OMR answer sheets.
  • An OMR sheet is a type of optical mark recognition (OMR) form that is used to capture data from handwritten or typed marks.
  • OMRjet offers a variety of features, including the ability to create custom question papers, scan OMR answer sheets, generate reports, and evaluate OMR answer sheets automatically.
